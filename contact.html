How to contact us

You want to talk to us personally or stay up to date with the most recent information?

For the most recent information you can join our Discord. Here you can find almost all the members and Community members. They're always ready to answer questions about games, events or the clan. You can also follow us on Twitter at @CCG_Yami or @CCGClan. Here we remind you of updates about certain games and about events.

How to join us

Interested in joining the clan?

Are you interested in joining? The only way to request a join; is to fill in the the form at the bottom of the page. Your request will be sent to our Management Team and they will review your application. You will get a reply in 5-7 work days.
